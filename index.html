BRAJ also known as Brij, is a region of Utter Pradesh in India, comprising the twin cities of Mathura and Vrindavan. It is not just a sacred land where lord Krishna was born and performed his cosmic leelas, but also a place of divine reminisces. The entire region together is most aptly christened Brajbhumi meaning the land of Lord Krishna.Embodied in a culturally ethereal spirit of the Lord, Brajbhumi forms a pilgrimage halo with temple cities like Mathura, Vrindavan, Govardhan, Gokul, Barsana under its vibrant folds.
